Eco-Friendly Living Tips

  • Say no to single-use plastics
  • Carry reusable bottles, bags & cutlery
  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances
  • Compost kitchen waste at home
  • Use public or shared transport

Sustainable Travel

  • Book green-certified stays in Goa
  • Respect local ecosystems and communities
  • Visit eco-tourism spots like wildlife sanctuaries
  • Travel off-season to reduce pressure

Youth & Community Projects

  • Beach cleanups by Ocean Guardians
  • Recycling workshops at local schools
